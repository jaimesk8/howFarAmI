How to find a friend

Now you're ready to connect with your friends, share your locations, and explore together on "How Far Am I?" It's as simple as that!

1. Generate your code

Click the "Generate Code" button to create your unique identifier. This code is your key to sharing your location with friends.

2. Share Your Code and Location

Share your generated code with your friends through messaging apps or in person. Ask your friends to enter your code into their "How Far Am I?" app.

3. Get a Friend Code

Ask your friends to generate their own friend codes within the app. Collect their friend codes and enter them in your app. Once connected, you can see each other's locations on the map.
